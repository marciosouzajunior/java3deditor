
Orbit Movement (Mouse):

Button 1 = Turn in orbit
Button 2 = Move the center
Scroll = Zoom
___________________________________

Rotation:

Delete, Pagedown = Rotate in Y axis
Home, End = Rotate in X axis
Insert, Pageup = Rotate in Z axis
___________________________________

Movement (Numeric keys):

4, 6 = left, right (X axis)
2, 8 = back, forward(Z axis)
7, 9 = top, down (Y axis)
___________________________________

Scale:

+ = increase
- = decrease
___________________________________

Non-uniform Scale:

A, D = X axis
W, S = Y axis
Q, E = Z axis
